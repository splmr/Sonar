ABOUT Sonr

We believe that there is so much more to finding music than likes or dislikes. Sonr allows you to find the songs that really stands out to you. Find music that feels right — swipe, listen and explore with Sonr.

What We Offer

Sonr's unique approach to music discovery puts the focus back on you. A speed-dating approach to finding songs you like, skip the ones you don't and provide detailed responses to tailor your recommendations. Feeling a song that comes up? Add it straight to your playlist and we'll do our best to give you more like it.

Better for Artists

The platform not only refines user recommendations through granular feedback but also enables artists to showcase the unique details that define their sound—helping listeners discover them beyond basic genres. Sonr puts the focus on the sound, not how many streams or followers the artist has. Our detailed recommendations off of mood, tempo and more mean that we can recommend music based off of the music.

Your Journey

Finding new music that resonates with you doesn't have to be a chore. Get started now with Sonr!

How It Works

● Like a dating app, but with music, and without the pick-up lines.

● Swipe left or right on snippets of songs, Swipe up and give us more detailed comments to better personalise your recommendations.

● You can also share straight to your favourite streaming platform from here!

● Swipe down if you're neither here nor there and skip to the next song.
